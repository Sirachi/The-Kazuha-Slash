Kazuha's Ult

By Sidd Sajan

Kazuha's ult has to be by far one of my personal favorite ults in the whole game. It's so unique and its name is just simply amazing. It's very helpful for anemo reactions. This with a 4 piece virdersent venerer set of artifacts, which can actually decrease the enemy's resistance to the elemental that was swirled. For example, if I use my keqing's first e skill which creates a lighting mark in front of her, it applies the enemy with electro. Then using kazuha's ult and/or e skill, you can swirl electro which in turn activates the artifact effect and then decreases the enemy resistance to electro by around 40%.(Damn imagine actually reading all this, like how bored are you? Go outside or something, smh) Then switching back to keqing and activating her second e skill in which she teleports to the lighting mark, she can do increased dmg.
